    Investors

    Understand yield, risk-adjusted returns, tax and residency pathways, and portfolio concentration before investing.

    Brokers

    Accelerate triage, match supply and demand, and surface deal blockers early with trusted AI copilots.

    Advisors

    Mixture-of-Experts mesh

    Specialized AI agents collaborate through a unified orchestration layer.

    Property discovery expert

    Matches user intent with property supply, neighborhood context, and lifestyle constraints.

    Valuation expert

    Blends comparable sales, market momentum, valuation bands, and location intelligence for each listing.

    RBI eligibility expert

    Maps candidate profiles to jurisdiction-specific residency-by-investment requirements and timelines.

    Insurance expert

    Generates coverage options, insurability signals, and ACORD-aligned data packages.

    Risk expert

    Scores fraud, climate, cyber, legal, and operational exposures with mitigation suggestions.

    Compliance expert

    Applies RBAC, MFA, KYC/AML, sanctions, privacy, records, and release gating before decisions are shown.

    Recommendation expert

    Ranks listings against user preferences, fairness checks, explainability rules, and cross-expert signals.

    Finance expert

    Supports affordability, leverage, cashflow, and scenario analysis for individual or institutional users.

    Compliance-by-design

    Security, privacy, resilience, and AI governance are embedded in every workflow.

    ISO/IEC 27001 ISO/IEC 27017 ISO/IEC 27701 SOC 2 Type 2 ISO/IEC 25010 ISO/IEC 5259 ISO 9241-210 ISO 31000 ISO 22301 ISO/IEC 42001 ACORD KYC/AML Sanctions

    Identity release gate

    Decisions are published only after privacy minimization, RBAC, MFA, KYC/AML, sanctions screening, and risk-threshold review.

    Audit evidence

    Every recommendation captures frontend profile signals, trust posture, models consulted, comparable sets, ranking explanations, policy versions, confidence, and time-stamped approvals.

    AI governance

    ISO/IEC 5259 data-quality controls and ISO/IEC 42001 AI-management controls govern valuation, ranking fairness, explainability, and human oversight.

    Resilience

    Active-active services, fallback experts, retriable workflows, and immutable event logs support business continuity and incident response.
