    Mixture-of-Experts mesh

    Specialized AI agents collaborate through unified recommendation and transaction orchestration.

    Property discovery expert

    Matches user intent with property supply, neighborhood context, and lifestyle constraints.

    Valuation expert

    Blends comparable sales, market momentum, valuation bands, and location intelligence for each listing.

    Pricing strategy expert

    Recommends opening offers, concession ladders, and walk-away thresholds based on market context.

    Negotiation expert

    Surfaces counterparty posture, concession sequencing, timing tactics, and escalation conditions.

    Document validation expert

    Checks diligence artifacts, signatures, disclosures, title evidence, and release-readiness gaps.

    Deal risk expert

    Scores workflow integrity, leverage, document exceptions, and counterparty risk with escalation guidance.

    Unified compliance & risk intelligence expert

    Continuously evaluates real-estate, payments, insurance, and residency workflows with AML/KYC, sanctions screening, audit logging, PCI-safe release controls, and ISO/IEC 27001 plus ISO 31000-aligned risk scoring.

    Payment intelligence expert

    Scores fraud probability, payer behavior, escrow conditions, and settlement reconciliation before money moves.

    Finance expert

    Supports affordability, leverage, cashflow, scenario analysis, and payment release suitability for individual or institutional users.

    Compliance-by-design

    Security, privacy, resilience, and AI governance are embedded in every workflow.

    ISO/IEC 27001 ISO/IEC 27017 ISO/IEC 27701 SOC 2 Type 2 ISO/IEC 25010 ISO/IEC 5259 ISO 9241-210 ISO 31000 ISO 22301 ISO/IEC 42001 PCI DSS ACORD KYC/AML Sanctions

    Identity release gate

    Decisions are published only after privacy minimization, RBAC, MFA, AML/KYC validation, sanctions screening, payment tokenization checks, continuous audit logging, and cross-workflow risk-threshold review.

    Audit evidence

    Every recommendation, payment event, insurance action, residency milestone, and deal-stage transition captures profile signals, token references, models consulted, document hashes, policy versions, confidence, risk scores, and time-stamped approvals.

    AI governance

    ISO/IEC 5259 data-quality controls and ISO/IEC 42001 AI-management controls govern valuation, ranking fairness, explainability, and human oversight.

    Resilience

    Active-active services, fallback experts, retriable workflows, immutable event logs, and reconciliation replay support business continuity aligned to ISO 22301.
